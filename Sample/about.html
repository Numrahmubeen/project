About

Computer World is dedicated to informing people about technology and its uses. Not only is Computer World a reliable source of technology basics, it also delivers helpful information that can keep people safe and aware in a world of quickly changing technology.


Computer World was founded with the purpose of giving visitors easy to understand information about the technology they use in personal and professional settings. The informative articles found on Computer.com are created by a team of knowledgeable industry professionals that are on the pulse of all things technology. The columnists and contributors at Computer.com are committed to making technology accessible for everyone with easy to follow articles that explain the specific technology and how it can be properly used. The valuable information produced by the Computer.com team is honest and reliable. Visitors can find a wide range of interesting and educational articles encompassing many different aspects of technology and its uses including business software, data services, computers and accessories, websites and hosting, personal software, cloud computing, security software, marketing software, internet and communications, tech support and networking.


The inquisitive team at Computer World is always researching and reviewing the latest technology. They are continuously adding new and engaging tech articles to Computer.com to keep visitors informed and up-to-date.
